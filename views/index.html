About Lyrong

There has been an enormous trend in the music industry. Sometimes it becomes a tedious job to find the correct lyrics of a song. The current trend of research on finding lyrics of your favorite song several variant of these songs algorithms have been developed. The current research is to study and develop algorithm that search for lyrics of song.

Lyrong is an online website that facilitates song lyrics and provides clean and refreshing experience to the user .It helps in finding the lyrics instantly.

It disintegrates all the useless information such as paid advertisement etc. It requires two sections for searching lyrics Artist’s name and the name of the song.

With the help of this system one can access songs’ lyrics anytime and anywhere. Moreover, the website is compatible with almost all the platforms. Another advantage is that it is very light weight and ad free which makes the website ideal.
